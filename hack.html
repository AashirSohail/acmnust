Judges Panel

Judges for this Hackathon are experts in their domains. Providing excellent mentorship for the period of this Hackathon. These are the people you need in your networking circles so don't miss your chance to register for HackACM'19.

Sadia Khurram

Cheif Customer Experience Officer Jazz

Sadia Khurram is the Chief Customer Experience Officer at Jazz, is the first entry in our introduction of the judging panel for HackACM '19. Sadia has previously worked in many senior positions with Mobilink, she also joined Careem as GM Lahore.

Saad Hamid

Google Developer Community Manager

Saad Hamid is a technology entrepreneur and a Global Shaper at World Economic Forum. Pakistan's first Google Developer Expert (GDE) and has been recognised by WEF as one of the Davos50 innovators in 2018.

Qarib Kazmi

Senior Software Engineer

Qarib Kazmi is a Senior Software Engineer in Bentley Systems Pakistan and has a great deal of experience in the computer software industry. He has managed and organized many tech events and hackathons earlier.

Muazzam Arslan

CEO Alfoze Technologies

An entrepreneur with 15+ years experience in Software/Telecom sector, Muazzam Arslan Bhatii is Founder & CEO of Alfoze Technologies. And member of the IT Policy Advisory Group of Islamabad Chamber of Commerce & Industry & the founder of Invoicellence, a cloud-based E-invoicing platform.
